📋 Filing Procedures
Pre-hearing briefs must be submitted at least 5 business days before the hearing date. Late submissions may not be entered into evidence without a continuance motion.
This office accepts electronic submissions via ERE. Physical exhibits should be faxed to (615) 736-7498 with a cover sheet referencing the hearing docket number.
Representative fee petitions must be filed within 60 days of a favorable decision. Contact Tammy Reeves for processing questions.
