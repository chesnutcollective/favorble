Case Summary

Client Marcus Williams sustained injuries when a City Transit Authority bus ran a red light at the intersection of Wilshire Blvd and Vermont Ave on October 14, 2025. Mr. Williams suffered a fractured left femur, herniated disc (L4-L5), and multiple soft tissue injuries requiring surgery and ongoing physical therapy. The bus driver, identified as Carlos Mendez (Employee #4782), was operating the vehicle during a double shift in violation of City Transit work-hour policies. Security camera footage from a nearby business corroborates the client's account.
